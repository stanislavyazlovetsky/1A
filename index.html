Arthur Morgan was born circa 1863
to Beatrice and Lyle Morgan in the northern US.
As a child, his mother died of unknown causes,
while his father was a petty criminal and outlaw.

In 1874, when Arthur was 11 years old, his father was arrested for larceny.

Morgan later witnessed his death and, despite a strained relationship with him,
still donned his hat and kept a picture of him.

You know, all that ever mattered to me was loyalty.
It was all I knew. It was all I ever believed in...
